Roommate List

Here is a collection of every roommate.

You can get to know a little bit about each person and have some fun facts!

Usage:

a) Press the "Add" button to add a roommate by filling in their name, birthday, hobbies and other important information.

b) After the roommate card is added, click the card to edit the information about your roommate. Remember to save your changes by clicking "save" button after editing!

c) There will also be a "Del" button on the right top corner if you are not going to be living with your roommate anymore!

d) Press "x" button on the right top corner if you accidentally started editing a roommate card!
